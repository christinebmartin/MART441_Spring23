Adventures of Glacier

Glacier is a fun loving, free spirit Akbash (turkish herding dog). She was adoped from the Flathead County Humane Society 4 years ago. She enjoys sitting as high as she can so she can look over and protect her "flock" . Weighing over 100 lbs, she's a big girl who sheds about a dog a day in hair.

Glacier loves adventures, so much so her owners had to get a special color that tracked where she went on her adventures.
